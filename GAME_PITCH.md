Shadow Quest – Game Pitch Document

Author: Hitesh
Course: CSCI 14 – Fall 2025
Game Type: Turn-Based RPG

Overview

Shadow Quest is a terminal-based, turn-based RPG where the player explores a 10×10 grid world, fights enemies, collects items, levels up, and defeats the final boss known as the Shadow Lord.

The game focuses on classic RPG gameplay inspired by old-school games like Dragon Quest and early Final Fantasy, designed specifically for terminal play using simple controls and text-based output.

Game Summary

The player starts in a small village and explores the surrounding world using WASD movement. Each move can trigger a random enemy encounter. During combat, the player chooses to attack, use items, or attempt to flee.

As enemies are defeated, the player gains experience, levels up, and becomes stronger. The goal is to reach the boss room and defeat the Shadow Lord to win the game.

Player Goal

Main Goal: Defeat the Shadow Lord and save the realm

Secondary Goals:

Level up your character

Collect items and gold

Explore the map safely and strategically

Core Mechanics

Grid-Based Exploration:
A 10×10 map where different terrain types affect movement

Random Encounters:
Enemies appear randomly while moving through the map

Turn-Based Combat:
Player and enemy take turns attacking
Player can attack, use items, or flee

Character Progression:
Gain experience, level up, and increase stats

Inventory System:
Store and use items like health potions

Save and Load System:
Allows the player to save progress and continue later

Why This Game Is Fun

Shadow Quest is simple to understand but still challenging. The player must manage health, items, and risk while exploring the map. Every decision matters, especially when deciding whether to keep exploring or return to save the game.

Estimated Playtime

Full Playthrough: 15–25 minutes

Replay Value: Different map layouts and player choices each run
